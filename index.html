TECHNORA'25

Department of Computer Science and Engineering - NICHE

Technora'25 is a technical fest featuring a variety of technical games. Participants from various colleges will have the opportunity to showcase their skills, compete in innovative challenges, and win exciting prizes, making it a thrilling event for tech enthusiasts looking to test their abilities and connect with like-minded peers.
