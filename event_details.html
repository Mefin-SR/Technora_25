TECHNORA'25

DataDrop(PaperPresentation)

Echoes of the Future in Every Slide

  • Max 2 members in a team
  • The paper should be original work and not presented previously
  • Presentation time will be 10 minutes
  • A prototype can also be displayed

CodeRush(CodePairing)

When Code Becomes Combat

  • Max 2 members per team
  • Each team will be given a program to debug
  • One member will be sitting with the timer, while the other member will debug the program
  • After a time limit of 5 minutes, the member will switch
  • Each round goes up to 20 minutes
  • The team with the fewest syntax errors wins the round

PixelForge(UI/UXDesign)

Forge Function into Form

  • Max 2 members per team
  • Participants will be given a common page to design(Ex, login page, social media fyp, food app home page)
  • The participants can use Figma or Canva
  • Pre-designed templates are not allowed, although participants can use references such as images to recreate a particular page
  • The judges will decide on the best UI and announce the winner

GridWars (Esports[Pubg/FreeFire])

Alt+F4 If You Fear Victory

  • 4 players per team.
  • The matches will be played on the campus.
  • Participation is restricted to individuals physically present on the campus.
  • The match type will be TDM.
  • The competition will follow an elimination round format.
  • Maintain fair play and respect opponents at all times during the competition.

CyberQuest404(Treasure Hunt)

Prize Not Found… Yet

  • 2 members per team
  • Each team will start with one clue
  • There will be a total of 5 clues
  • There will be a maximum of 5 teams
  • Each team will have their colored clues
  • A team should only claim the clue that is in their color
  • First team to find the 5th clue wins

PixelHunt(Photography)

Caught in 4K… and a little JPEG too

  • Both cameras and mobile phones are allowed to take photos.
  • The time limit will be 1 hour.
  • Only photographs of nature scenarios are allowed, such as landscapes, insects, birds, etc…
  • No photo editing or manipulation is allowed. Photographs must be presented in their original form.
  • Photographs should be sent to the student coordinator's email in JPEG format.

CrashCode(ShipWreck)

Bold of you to assume I had a point

  • It is an individual competition
  • Two MCs will be assigned as captains
  • The MCs will decide on the theme of the debate on the spot
  • The debate will be one-on-one
  • At the end of the debate, the MCs will decide who will advance to the next round
  • The fixtures will be decided based on the number of participants on the spot

HoloRhythm(GroupDance)

Moves So Clean, Even the Code's Jealous

  • Maximum 5 members for a group.
  • Performance must be 3-5 minutes.
  • Teams are free to choose their music for the performance.
  • Teams must provide their own music playback devices.

SynthwaveFlow(SoloDance)

Every step is a signal. Every move, a message.

  • Individual participation
  • Performance must be 3-5 minutes
  • Participants are free to choose their music for the performance.
  • Participants must provide their own music playback devices.
